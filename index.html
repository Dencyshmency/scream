B2B Marketplace
TradeToWorld (TTW) is a B2B platform where Sellers display their products and Buyers visualize them, selecting wished items and the two parties start a direct communication to negotiate a trade deal.
So, like all major global B2B marketplaces, TTW provides a matching between international Sellers and Buyers.
All-in-one Export Platform
However, TTW goes far beyond its scope to provide limitless opportunities and global visibility to your Selling Company. As a matter of fact, the platform helps SMEs (Small to Medium Enterprises) in all their trade journey. Subscribing as Seller, you will:
Digitize your company data and catalogues
Create an attractive showroom of your products
Experience simplified trade processes and tasks
Receive professional support for any trade need upon request
Communicating Worldwide
Nowadays, Buyers and Sellers dialogue with salesforce, service companies, trade partners, logistics carriers, bank officers, etc. using multiple communication channels: email, calls, video calls, chat or text messages, through a wide range of platforms.
TradeToWorld simplifies it, letting all of them can use, free of charge, a unique gateway: TTW Interactive Room.
By App (smartphones, tablets) or Marketplace page (desktop), Selle rs and Buyers can call, chat, video conferencing and set up webinars and live demos in one unique place!
Engineering Trade Processes
TradeToWorld is a newborn marketplace. Its early stage allows the platform to adopt the newest technologies to digitize trade physical activities and dramatically simplify and automate all trade processes: documentation, communication, exchange of data and information among all trade players.
Leveraging the extensive experience of the founders in international trade, and the great automation in managing trade processes through new technologies, such as:
Artificial Intelligence
For the marketplace internal search engine optimization, scouting, classifying and reorganizing trade data in the world net
Internet of Things
Logistics data and shipment tracking
Blockchain Technology
Documentation flow and payment transactions
Promoting Sustainability
TradeToWorld, toward sustainability as key factor in international trade and supply chains
The marketplace company, TradeToWorld Marketplace Corporation, has been establish as “Benefit Corporation”. This means that we are inner engaged in creating a sustainable company in all means: versus our employees, stakeholders, community and environment. No compromise.
But, as global gateway of companies, we understand how our role can take a much wider role: promoting sustainable business, asking our corporate users to adopt the change and to work to constantly improve their societal profile. How?
TTW is the first marketplace in the world which embeds in its Search Engine parameters such as supplier or product sustainable ranking. And companies with a good score get promoted by higher visibility and free services.
